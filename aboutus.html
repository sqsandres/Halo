About Us:



Manufacturers of custom heating, ventilating, air conditioning and process piping for industrial and recreational uses as well as private applications.
Backed by 40 years of industry experience in the heating, ventilation and air conditioning arena, Marx has been a part of the construction of many notable facilities.

Our awareness of all aspects of construction work, such as staffing and management, schedules and budgets and mandated safety codes and regulations have made us leaders in our industry. Our qualified team of professionals is comprised of mechanical construction engineers, seasoned estimators and skilled project managers and foremen who have extensive backgrounds in their fields.

Together, they ensure the complete success of your construction project. Our list of satisfied customers range from small companies to large industrial applications, with much of our work procured from referrals. The breadth of our completed projects include hotel chains, office buildings, stadiums and arenas, hospitals, shopping centers, pharmaceutical laboratories, and schools.

No matter what the scope of the work entails, Marx Sheet Metal & Mechanical, Inc. gives the same attention to all projects.



Generations of Service


For three generations, Marx Sheet Metal & Mechanical and Mechanical, Inc. has been a family run business

Built on pride- pride in our craftsmanship in constructing some of the region’s finest HVAC products in the marketplace, pride in meeting or exceeding deadlines without compromising quality, and pride in our organization.

Company founder, Donald Marx Sr. bent his first sheet of metal in 1954 and is still at the helm of the business and oversees the daily operations. With the addition of four sons and three grandchildren now actively involved in the business, we are poised to continue our tradition of providing excellence to the community.

Marx is proactive in our approach to safety. Our employees follow strict quality assurance guidelines and exercise quality control throughout every aspect of the job while always keeping safety in mind. Whether in the shop or in the field, our employees work together for the common goal of completing a job incident free while being on time and within budget.



Our Facility

Marx Sheet Metal and Mechanical, Inc. is a complete mechanical and HVAC contractor with a full in-house production facility. Our 35,000 square foot plant is one of the most modern shops in the state Pennsylvania. Located in Wilkes-Barre, we are central to the market areas of Northeastern and Central Pennsylvania

Marx’s capabilities cover the entire spectrum of duct and pipe manufacturing. We are flexible in custom designing systems to meet the individual needs of our diverse customer base. From the earliest pre-planning stages, Marx will devote full attention to the most minute detail of the project. Our service department will be available to answer your questions and give you up-to-the-minute status reports about your project.

Our shop can produce an average of 70,000 pounds of galvanized/ stainless/ aluminum ductwork per week. Using the latest in pipe manufacturing technology, our CAD system produces spool pieces with a numbering system to go directly to the site.

Our qualified team of professionals is comprised of mechanical construction engineers, seasoned estimators, and skilled project managers and foremen who have extensive backgrounds in their fields. Together, they ensure the complete success of your construction project.

Our fully qualified mechanics are skilled in both ductwork and pipe manufacturing processes. Some of services and capabilities include:

• Computerized estimating • Automated shearing and rolling capabilities • Certified welders • Plasma fitting cutting tables • Air compressed hammering systems • Construction management • Customized critical path scheduling • Budget cost tracking

Marx Sheet Metal & Mechanical Inc. belongs to the following such trade organizations: • MCAA (Mechanical Contractors of America Association) • NCPW (National Certified Pipe Welders) • SMACNA (Sheet Metal & Air Conditioning National Association)
