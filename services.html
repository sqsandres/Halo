Marx Sheet Metal and Mechanical, Inc. is prepared to handle singular aspects of a project or the entire process of designing a system to meet your needs. We can design, manufacture, install, and maintain any heating, ventilation, air conditioning, or piping systems for commercial or industrial applications.
BIM Sheet Metal HVAC Piping Plumbing Manufacturing Design Maintenance Industrial

Marx Sheet Metal and Mechanical, Inc. utilizes modeling softwares such as Revit, SysQue, Autodesk Fabrication, and Navisworks for clash detection and coordination. BIM 360 is utilized to bring BIM to the field using iPads and BIM Boxes for the field personnel.
